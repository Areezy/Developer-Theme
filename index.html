Chrome State App - Save all your tabs easily

This is a chrome extension that aims to replace traditional bookmarks. Bookmarks in browsers are tedious and can get difficult to find as different folders grow. Using this extension, you could save all your tabs in a batch script, rename them as you see fit and lauch them to recover all the tabs you had "bookmarked".
